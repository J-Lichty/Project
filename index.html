Controls

  • WASD - Move
  • Mouse - Shoot
  • Hold Shift to aim
  • F - Spawn chicken
  • G - Spawn terrain objects
  • H - Spawn resources
  • 1,2,3,4 - Change skin
  • 5,6,7,8 - Change gun
